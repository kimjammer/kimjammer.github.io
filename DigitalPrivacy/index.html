Digital Privacy

You need better digital privacy! Yes You!

(Especially if you are in the USA)

There is more to digital privacy than not posting your social security number online!

Why?

You should know that digital privacy is very important. It’s not just for people who want to hide things. Identity thieves will try (and succeed!) to steal identities from people on the internet, big data leaks can reveal private personal information to anyone in the world,and it is impossible to know all of the different intentions of the people on the internet.

How do I protect my digital privacy? [1]

  1. Secure your accounts and use a password manager to help you remember (Like LastPass)
  2. Get an anti-tracker extension for your browser (Like uBlock Origin)
  3. Update your Software and programs (When your phone or computer says "Update available", don't ignore it!)
  4. Stop privacy-invading laws and pressure adding privacy-protection laws (How? Look Below ↓ )

I want to do something about Digital Privacy!

You can take action by pressuring your local representatives to take legislative steps! Let's make the US catch up to the EU in Digital Privacy Laws! The Electronic Frontier Foundation also provides great ways to tell your representatives this.
